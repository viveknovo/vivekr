About Us

Nehru Arts & Science College is situated in Kasaragod in Kerala state of India. Established in 1968, it is accredited from NAAC and it is affiliated to Kannur University. b'Nehru Arts & Science College', Kasaragod offers 15 courses across 4 streams namely Science, Arts, Commerce and Banking, Agriculture and across 6 degrees like BSc, BA, B.Com, MA, MSc.Hostel facility is not available for its students.
